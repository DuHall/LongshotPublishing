Step 3 - Promote

Step 3 will involve our Marketing team at Longshot publishing to give your book the exposure that it deserves. Your assigned professional will create a press kit that will be released on our website for easy sharing across social media and other sources.

A Press Kit consists of:

    Synopsis of the book
    Copy of the public release
    High Resolution image of the cover

Your professional will then submit your book to Books in print, which is a tool used by many libraries and book sellers. Our Marketing team will then use the power of Search Engine Optimization (SEO) to increase visibility of your book across the Internet.
